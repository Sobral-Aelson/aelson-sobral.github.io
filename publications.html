Paper Publication list

Regularity estimates for nonlocal equations with an asymptotic property (SNPDEA)

We study the regularity theory of nonlocal equations with an asymptotic property, allowing to obtain improved regularity results. In collaboration with

  • Disson dos Prazeres

Cordes-Nirenberg type results for nonlocal equations with deforming kernels (Submitted)

We develop a program to obtain regularity results for nonlocal equations whose kernels deform in a rate comparable to global solutions to the Monge-Ampere equation. In collaboration with

  • Disson dos Prazeres
  • José Miguel Urbano

Regularity in diffusion models with gradient activation (Submitted)

We develop the regularity theory for solutions to highly degenerate elliptic equations and describe how it connects with different trends of research. In collaboration with

  • Damião Araújo
  • Eduardo Teixeira

On free boundary problems shaped by oscillatory singularities (Submitted)

We study free boundary problems that resemble the classical Alt-Philips problem, but allowing the singularity to vary. In collaboration with

  • Damião Araújo
  • José Miguel Urbano
  • Eduardo Teixeira

Higher regularity of solutions to fully nonlinear elliptic equations (Submitted)

We obtain higher regularity estimates for fully nonlinear elliptic (singular) equations that, surprinsingly, surpasses the natural threshold. In collaboration with

  • Ginaldo Sá
  • Thialita Nascimento
  • Eduardo Teixeira

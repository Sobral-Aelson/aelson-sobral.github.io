About

I got my B.S in applied mathematics in 2018 and M.SC in pure mathematics by the Federal University of Sergipe under the supervision of Disson Soares dos Prazeres. In the same year I joined the Federal University of Paraíba as a PhD student, which I'm about to defend, under the advising of Damião Araújo and co-advising of Eduardo Teixeira (UCF).

Research

My research interests are mainly in free boundary problems associated to viscosity solutions of degenerate/singular equations. Moreover, qualitative properties, as ABP estimates and Harnack-type inequality, associated to solutions of ellipt, parabolic and nonlocal equations has caught my attention in the past years.
